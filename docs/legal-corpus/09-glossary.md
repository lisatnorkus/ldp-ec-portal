# Glossary

Terminology with source citations. Where a term has multiple definitions (bylaws-side vs operational-side vs UI-side), all are listed with the context for each.

This glossary leans heavily on the formal definitions in `[KDP Art. I.B]` and the operational vocabulary established in portal CLAUDE.md.

## A

**Ad Hoc Committee** — A committee created by the County Chair `[LJCDP §11.6]` that is not in the §26 standing list. Persists until rescinded. Current ad hoc committees (April 2026): Branding, Endorsement Process, Platform.

**Appeal** — A formal challenge to a Convention election or CEC decision, governed by `[KDP Art. V]`. Day counts vary by source:
- Precinct Convention/Election: 3 days `[KDP Art. V.A]`
- LD or County Convention: 7 days `[KDP Art. V.B]`
- State Convention: 7 days `[KDP Art. V.C]`
- CEC decision under Article III: 5 days `[KDP Art. V.D]`
- All other: 5 days `[KDP Art. V.E]`
- Legal action venue if appeals exhausted: Franklin County Circuit Court `[KDP Art. V.F]`

**At-Large** — A position elected to serve an entire area rather than one of its subdivisions `[KDP Art. I.B.b]`. JCDEC has two At-Large categories:
1. **18 countywide At-Large members** elected jointly by LD Chairs + Vice Chairs at close of LD Convention per `[KDP Art. II.D.c]` and `[KDP Art. III.D.b.c.v]`. Proportional to Dem registration per LD. Two must be 35 or under.
2. **1 At-Large from Dem elected officials** per `[LJCDP §2.1]`, `[LJCDP §3.1]`
3. **(Expired)** SCEC-mandated 2000-2004 At-Large — still listed in `[LJCDP §3.1]`; should be removed by amendment.

Pre-2025: LJCDP had a different LD-specific At-Large model under §§4.4, 6.6, 8 — superseded by KDP. See `11-known-drift.md`.

## B

**Bylaws Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Owns drift catalog (`11-known-drift.md`), reviews proposed amendments, recommends bylaw updates to full CEC. Counterpart at SCEC level: `[KDP Art. IV.O]`.

## C

**Call to Convention** — The formal notice issued by SCEC (or by SCEC Chair for single-county reorgs per `[KDP Art. III.D.d]`) setting the date, time, and process for Conventions. The Call also specifies delegate apportionment and any rules specific to the cycle.

**Candidate Recruitment Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Identifies and recruits Democrats to run for elected office in Jefferson County.

**CEC** — County Executive Committee. The KDP term for what JCDEC calls itself (and which JCDEC's bylaws also use). `[KDP Art. I.B.d]`: "the committee that oversees County Party affairs."

In user-facing portal copy, use **LDPEC** (LDP Executive Committee) per portal CLAUDE.md content rules. Cite as "CEC" only in bylaws references.

**CEC member** — A voting or non-voting member of the County Executive Committee. Voting membership per `[LJCDP §3.1]`. See also: **LDPEC member**.

**Celebration of Democracy Dinner** — LDP annual signature event (spring). **Renamed from "Wendell Ford Dinner." Never use the old name in any LDP deliverable.** Run by Events Committee subcommittee. Website may still show the old name; the rename is official.

**Charter** (DNC Charter) — The foundational governing document of the Democratic Party of the United States. Articles I–X plus Preamble and Resolution of Adoption. Amended March 12, 2022. Verbatim at `/docs/bylaws/dnc-charter-bylaws-2022-full-text.md`.

**Communications Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]` (where it's called "Communication"). Beth Thorpe = Communications Chair (NEVER "Communications Director"). Owns LDP public communications.

**Convention Chair** — The presiding officer at a Convention. A Party Officer at the applicable Convention Level per `[KDP Art. II.B.g]`. If unavailable, appointed by the County Party Chair (Precinct/LD) or State Party Chair (County/CD).

**Convention Levels** — The five levels of party reorganization per `[KDP Art. II.B.d]`:
1. Precinct
2. Legislative District (counties with 7+ LDs only)
3. County (counties with 6 or fewer LDs only)
4. Congressional District
5. State

Jefferson County uses LD Conventions, not County Conventions.

**County Chair** — Chair of the CEC. Elected by LD Chairs after Reorganization `[LJCDP §10.1]`. Must be any Democrat registered to vote in Jefferson County. Need not be a newly-elected LD Chair `[KDP Art. III.D.b.c.iii]`. Removable without cause by majority of voting CEC `[LJCDP §16.1]`.

**County Convention** — Convention held in counties with 6 or fewer legislative districts, the purpose of which is to elect Party Officials `[KDP Art. I.B.c]`. **Not used in Jefferson County** — JeffCo has 7+ LDs and uses LD Conventions.

**County Delegate** — Delegate elected at a County Convention to attend the State Convention. `[KDP Art. I.B.e]`. Not applicable to JeffCo (which uses LD-level delegate elections).

**County Executive Committee** — See CEC.

**County Vice Chair** — Elected by LD Chairs after the County Chair election `[LJCDP §10.1]`, `[LJCDP §10.2]`. Must be **opposite sex** of the Chair and must be a **newly-elected LD Chair** `[LJCDP §10.2]`, `[KDP Art. III.D.b.c.iii]`. Removable without cause by majority of voting CEC `[LJCDP §18.1]`. Vacancy may be automatically declared if a person of same sex as VC is elected mid-term to serve out an unexpired Chair term `[LJCDP §19.1]`.

## D

**Defined Terms** — The formal definitions section of KDP bylaws, at `[KDP Art. I.B]`. Sub-items (a) through (n).

**Delegate Selection Plan** — The cycle-specific plan adopted by SCEC and approved by DNC for selecting delegates to the Democratic National Convention. **Supersedes the KDP Bylaws in case of conflict** `[KDP Art. II.H.b]`. Drafted by KDP staff before each presidential cycle.

**Dems at the Downs** — LDP annual signature event (early November, post-election). Run by Events Committee subcommittee.

**DNC** — Democratic National Committee. National party body. `[DNC Charter Art. III]`.

**DNC Charter** — Foundational governing document. See **Charter**.

## E

**Eligibility to Run** — Any Democrat registered to vote within the applicable Convention Level `[KDP Art. II.B.c]`. Plus residency requirement for JeffCo LD positions per `[LJCDP §4.3]`.

**Eligibility to Vote** — Varies by Convention Level:
- Precinct Convention: Any Democrat residing AND registered in precinct `[KDP Art. II.C.b]`
- LD Convention: Only duly-elected Precinct Committee members from that LD `[KDP Art. II.D.b]`
- County Convention: Only duly-elected PC members `[KDP Art. II.E.b]`
- CD Convention: County Delegates `[KDP Art. II.F.b]`
- State Convention: State Delegates `[KDP Art. II.G.b]`

**Endorsement** — In Louisville context, the formal LDP-endorsed candidate in a nonpartisan race (Mayor or Metro Council per HB 388). 60% threshold via secret ballot ElectionRunner, no proxies. See `06-endorsement-process.md`.

**Endorsement Process Committee** — Ad hoc committee (not standing under §26). Owns the endorsement process. See `06-endorsement-process.md`.

**Events Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Owns the three signature events (Celebration of Democracy Dinner, Women Deliver Democracy, Dems at the Downs).

**Executive Committee** (DNC-level) — The smaller, more frequently-meeting body that handles DNC affairs between full DNC meetings. `[DNC Charter Art. IV]`, `[DNC Bylaws Art. III]`. Meets ≥4×/year. Distinct from the JCDEC CEC.

**Ex-officio** — Latin: "by virtue of office." A position held by virtue of holding another office. Examples in JCDEC:
- Secretary and Treasurer: ex-officio non-voting members of CEC `[LJCDP §13.1]`, `[LJCDP §14.1]`
- County Chair on all committees: ex-officio voting member `[LJCDP §11.7]`

## F

**Facilities Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Manages LJCDP HQ. Carolyn Benedict = Facilities Chair.

**Fiduciary Relationship** — Each LD Chair, LD VC, and At-Large has this relationship with the CEC per `[LJCDP §2.2]`. Coupled with the NDA requirement.

**Finance Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Adopts Fiscal Policy, reviewed annually in January `[LJCDP §26.2]`. Oversees all purchases per `[LJCDP §26.3]`. Mike Ward = Finance Chair.

**Fiscal Policy** — Adopted by Finance Committee, reviewed annually in January `[LJCDP §26.2]`. Specific text not currently in this corpus; should be committed to `04-jcdec-standing-rules.md`.

**Floor nomination** — A nomination made from the floor at a Convention. Permitted at any Convention per `[KDP Art. II.B.i.ii]`. Permitted at LD Convention for LD Chair, LD VC, LD At-Large per `[LJCDP §4.2.1]`.

**Franklin County Circuit Court** — Legal jurisdiction and venue for any legal action arising under KDP Bylaws after appeals are exhausted `[KDP Art. V.F.a]`.

## G

**General Provisions** (KDP) — `[KDP Art. I]`. Contains 18 lettered sections (A–R) including defined terms, anti-discrimination, primary endorsement prohibition, dismissal procedures, Robert's Rules fallback, and the now-sunset Art. I.R one-time 2025 shift.

## H

**Headquarters Maintenance / Headquarters Purchase** — Restricted donation designations per `[LJCDP §14.5]`. Treasurer maintains complete and separate accounting; donations spent only on those activities.

**HQ** — Louisville-Jefferson County Democratic Party Headquarters. Physical location where filings are made `[LJCDP §4.2]` and where proxies must be present by meeting start `[LJCDP §23.3]`.

## J

**JCDEC** — Jefferson County Democratic Executive Committee. The legal/bylaws name. In user-facing portal copy, use **LDPEC** per portal CLAUDE.md.

**Jefferson County Democratic Women's Club** — Sanctioned by majority CEC vote, 6 months after application `[LJCDP §3.4]`. President is voting CEC member `[LJCDP §3.1]`. Multiple sanctioned clubs share 1 vote `[LJCDP §3.2]`. Jessica Haggy = current JCDWC President AND Volunteer Chair.

## K

**KDP** — Kentucky Democratic Party. State party body. `[KDP Art. I.B.a]`. Headquartered at PO Box 694, Frankfort, KY 40602.

**KDP Bylaws** — Ratified by State Convention June 26, 2021. Last amended by SCEC March 1, 2025. Articles I–VII. Verbatim at `/docs/bylaws/kdp-bylaws-2025-full-text.md`.

**KY Const. § 117** — KY Constitutional provision making judicial races nonpartisan. Anchor for treating Circuit Court, District Court, Court of Appeals, and KY Supreme Court races as nonpartisan.

## L

**LD** — Legislative District. Always cite as "LD41" not "HD41" or "District 41" per portal CLAUDE.md. JeffCo has 18 LDs wholly or partially within county boundaries.

**LD Chair** — Chair of the Legislative District Committee. Elected at LD Convention by Precinct Committee members `[KDP Art. II.D]`. 4-year term. Convention Chair at LD-level Conventions in practice `[KDP Art. II.B.g]`.

**LD Convention** — Convention held in counties with 7+ LDs `[KDP Art. I.B.f]`. Elects LD Chair + Vice Chair (different genders) and delegates to CD + State Conventions. Eligible voters: duly-elected Precinct Committee members from that LD.

**LD Vice Chair** — Vice Chair of Legislative District Committee. Elected at LD Convention `[KDP Art. II.D]`. Becomes voting CEC member only when LD Chair absent `[KDP Art. III.D.b.c]`.

**LDP** — Louisville Democratic Party. Brand name used in user-facing copy. Formal names: Louisville-Jefferson County Democratic Party (LJCDP), Jefferson County Democratic Party (JCDP). Use "LDP" in UI per portal CLAUDE.md.

**LDP Executive Committee Portal** (ldp-ec-portal) — Internal web tool for 56+ LDPEC members. Audience EC-only. Distinct from public louisvilledems.com. Live URL: https://ldp-ec-portal.vercel.app.

**LDPEC** — LDP Executive Committee. User-facing portal term for what bylaws call the JCDEC/CEC.

**LDPEC member** — A voting or non-voting member of the LDPEC: 4 countywide officers, 18 LD Chairs, 18 LD Vice Chairs, 18 At-Large members, plus Young Dems and Women's Club presidents who hold voting seats. ~56+ total.

**Legislative District** — See LD.

**LJCDP** — Louisville-Jefferson County Democratic Party. Formal name of the local party `[LJCDP §1.1]`. Used in bylaws references but not in user-facing portal copy.

**LJCDP Bylaws** — Current ratified text. Sections 1–30. Verbatim at `/docs/bylaws/ljcdp-bylaws-full-text.md`.

**Louisville Metro Council** — Nonpartisan as of 2026 per HB 388. Eligible for LDP endorsement per the endorsement process. Top-2 advance regardless of primary share. See `06-endorsement-process.md`.

**Louisville Metro Mayor** — Nonpartisan as of 2026 per HB 388. Eligible for LDP endorsement. Top-2 advance.

## N

**National Committee** — See DNC.

**NDA** — Non-Disclosure Agreement. Required at start of term for LD Chairs, LD VCs, At-Large members per `[LJCDP §2.2]`. Plus any subsequent NDA required by the local party.

**Nominating Committee** — A committee constituted under `[KDP Art. VI]` to select a replacement nominee for a special election when a Democratic state-level seat is vacated mid-term. Structure varies by district type — see `02-kdp-bylaws.md` Article VI section.

**Non-Voting Members** — CEC: Secretary, Treasurer (ex-officio per `[LJCDP §13.1]`, `[LJCDP §14.1]`). SCEC: KDP Exec Director, Legal Counsel, Treasurer, all KY Statewide Constitutional Officers, all KY Dem US Congresspeople and Senators, up to 3 additional ex-officio per `[KDP Art. IV.F]`.

## P

**Party Leader** — `[KDP Art. I.B.h]`: A Democrat who holds elected statewide office, a Democratic member of KY's US Senate/Congressional delegation, a Democrat in the KY General Assembly, the KDP Chair, the KDP Executive Director, or any voting SCEC member who is not Chair or Vice Chair.

**Party Officer** — `[KDP Art. I.B.j]`: A Chair, Vice Chair, Treasurer, or Secretary of the SCEC, CEC, LD Committee, or Precinct Committee.

**Party Official** — `[KDP Art. I.B.g]`: A member of a Precinct Committee, CEC, or LD Committee who is NOT a Chair, Vice Chair, Secretary, Treasurer, or member of a Nominating Committee.

**Party Representative** — `[KDP Art. I.B.i]`: A member who sits on the SCEC in either ex-officio status or as a representative of an affiliated organization, council, or caucus.

**PC** — Precinct Captain. Working term in JeffCo per portal CLAUDE.md. State bylaws say "Precinct Committee member" `[KDP Art. II.C]`. Use "PC" in user-facing copy; cite "Precinct Committee member" in bylaws references.

**Platform Committee** — Ad hoc committee (April 2026). Owns LDP platform development.

**Power Base** — Strategy-tag friendly name for **PRIMARY** precincts (Democrats win by 20+ points). Per portal CLAUDE.md strategy vocabulary. Use friendly name in user-facing copy.

**Precinct Captain** — See PC.

**Precinct Chair** — One member of the newly-elected Precinct Committee, chosen by the attendees at the Precinct Convention `[KDP Art. II.C.c]`. Coordinates the Committee's activities.

**Precinct Committee** — Two people of different genders + a youth (any gender) from the precinct. Primary Party Officials responsible for organizing and building Democratic power within the precinct `[KDP Art. III.B.b]`. Also serve as delegates to LD or County Convention.

**Precinct Convention** — Convention to elect Precinct Committee members. Eligible voters: any Democrat residing and registered in the precinct `[KDP Art. II.C]`.

**Pre-filing window** — For LD Chair, LD VC, LD At-Large: October 1 of the year prior to quadrennial Reorganization through 5pm on the final filing date of the Reorganization year `[LJCDP §4.2.1]`. Optional; floor nomination at LD Convention also permitted.

**Primary Party Officials** — Per `[KDP Art. III.B.b]`, Precinct Committee members are "the primary Party Officials responsible for organizing and building Democratic power within its precinct."

**Proxy** — A written or electronic authorization for one CEC member to vote on behalf of another for a specific meeting. Permitted for general business but NOT for election or dismissal of any Party officer `[LJCDP §23.1]`, `[KDP Art. III.A.a]`. Proxy must be present at LJCDP HQ by meeting start `[LJCDP §23.3]`. Young Dems / Women's Club president CANNOT assign proxy per `[LJCDP §23.2]` (stricter than KDP).

## Q

**Quadrennial Reorganization** — The four-year cycle of party reorganization. Now on presidential-year cadence per `[KDP Art. II.A]` (2028, 2032, 2036, …).

**Quorum** — Minimum attendance required to conduct business. LJCDP CEC: 40% of LD Chairs + LD VCs + LD At-Large, in person or by proxy `[LJCDP §22.1]`. Proxies do NOT count for officer election/dismissal `[LJCDP §22.1.1]`.

## R

**Reorganization** — `[KDP Art. I.B.m]`: The process in which Precinct Committees, LD Committees, CECs, and SCEC elect new members and officers. Cadence: every 4 years on presidential-election years per `[KDP Art. II.A]`.

**Robert's Rules of Order** — Parliamentary procedure manual applied as fallback at every level per `[LJCDP §28.1]`, `[KDP Art. I.Q]`, `[DNC Charter Art. IX, §14]`. Cite as `[Robert's Rules §X:Y]` referring to Newly Revised, 12th ed.

## S

**Sanctioning** (Women's/Young Dems Clubs) — By majority vote of voting CEC, 6 months after application `[LJCDP §3.4]`. Clubs of presidents serving at bylaw adoption are presently sanctioned `[LJCDP §3.4.1]`.

**SCEC** — State Central Executive Committee. The KDP state-level body. `[KDP Art. I.B.k]`, `[KDP Art. IV]`. ~80+ members from various sources. Meets ≥1×/quarter `[KDP Art. IV.B]`.

**Sleeper Dem** — A registered Democrat who shows up in November general elections but skips Democratic primaries. Per portal CLAUDE.md targeting vocabulary.

**Standing Committee** — A committee listed in `[LJCDP §26.1]` (bylaws-side: 10 listed) and persisting as a recognized CEC committee. Practice runs 8 (drift catalog in `08-committee-structure.md` and `11-known-drift.md`).

**State Delegate** — `[KDP Art. I.B.l]`: A Democrat elected at either an LD Convention or County Convention whose role is to elect SCEC members from the State Delegates' Congressional District and at-large SCEC members.

## T

**Tellers** — Vote-counters at a Convention. Appointed by Convention Chair upon request `[KDP Art. II.B.h]`.

**Term** — 4 years for all Party Officers and Party Officials, presidential-year to presidential-year, unless death/removal/resignation `[LJCDP §4.5]`, `[LJCDP §10.3]`, `[KDP Art. II.B.b]`.

**Tie vote** — At Convention: immediate runoff; if runoff tied, coin flip `[KDP Art. II.B.i.iv]`. At CEC: broken by vote of SCEC `[LJCDP §24.1]`, `[KDP Art. III.D.j]`.

**Training Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]`. Trains LDPEC members on roles.

**Treasurer** — Ex-officio non-voting CEC member appointed by County Chair within 30 days of Chair's election `[LJCDP §11.3]`. Keeps all financial records, prepares monthly financial statement to CEC `[LJCDP §14.3]`, files KREF + FEC reports per `[KDP Art. III.D.e.iii]`.

## V

**Volunteer Committee** — Standing committee per `[LJCDP §26.1]` (where it's called "Volunteering"). Owns volunteer pipeline. Jessica Haggy = Volunteer Chair AND Jefferson County Democratic Women's Club President.

**Voting Members of CEC** — `[LJCDP §3.1]`: LD Chairs + LD Vice Chairs (from same district as Chair) + 1 At-Large from Dem elected officials + (expired 2000-2004 SCEC mandate) + 18 At-Large per LD + Women's Club president(s) + Young Dems Club president(s).

## W

**Waiver** — A permission from the SCEC Chair (and sometimes Appellate and Waiver Committee) to deviate from a bylaw or convention requirement. Process at `[KDP Art. II.B.e]` (date/time waivers) and `[KDP Art. V.G]`, `[KDP Art. V.H]` (rules waivers). Emergency waivers permitted ≥1 hour before convention via phone/text/email for acts of God or declared emergencies.

**Wendell Ford Dinner** — Legacy name for what is now the **Celebration of Democracy Dinner**. Never use this name in any LDP deliverable. The rename is official; the website may still show the old name.

**Women Deliver Democracy** — LDP annual signature event (August). Run by Events Committee subcommittee.

## Y

**Young Democrats Club** (Jefferson County) — Sanctioned by majority CEC vote per `[LJCDP §3.4]`. President is voting CEC member `[LJCDP §3.1]`. Multiple sanctioned clubs share 1 vote `[LJCDP §3.3]`. President cannot assign proxy per `[LJCDP §23.2]`.

**Youth** — `[KDP Art. I.B.n]`: Any registered Democrat under the age of 40. Used in convention composition (e.g., Precinct Committee includes 1 youth of any gender per `[KDP Art. II.C.a]`; 1 youth per CD on SCEC per `[KDP Art. II.F.a]`).
