# LJCDP Bylaws — Index

Louisville-Jefferson County Democratic Party Bylaws. Governs JCDEC operations to the extent not inconsistent with KDP or DNC `[LJCDP §5.3]`. Where LJCDP conflicts with KDP, KDP governs and the drift is flagged for the Bylaws Committee.

**Verbatim source:** `/docs/bylaws/ljcdp-bylaws-full-text.md` — 30 sections, current ratified text

**Note on numbering:** LJCDP uses Section numbers (e.g., §22.1) rather than Article/Section pairs. Cite as `[LJCDP §X.Y]`.

## Drift in LJCDP — see `11-known-drift.md`

**Surface but do not silently resolve:**

1. **§3.1 — Expired 2000-2004 SCEC-mandated At-Large seat.** Still appears in voting member list. Should be removed by amendment.
2. **§4.4 / §6.6 / §8 — LD At-Large model pre-2025.** §4.4 says "elected by LD Chairs … within 30 days of Reorganization." §6.6 says LD Chair elects one LD At-Large per LD. §8 describes LD-specific duties. **All three together describe the pre-2025 LD-specific At-Large structure.** Superseded by `[KDP Art. II.D.c]` + `[KDP Art. III.D.b.c.v]` (18 countywide elected jointly at close of LD Convention). KDP governs.
3. **§23.2 — Young Dems / Women's Club proxy restriction.** Stricter than `[KDP Art. III.A.a.ii]`. May be permissible as a more restrictive local rule (not inconsistent with KDP) OR may be drift. Surfaced for Bylaws Committee.
4. **§26.1 — Standing committee list.** Lists 10 standing committees. Practice (April 2026): 8 standing + 3 ad hoc. Naming drifted ("Volunteering" → Volunteer, "Communication" → Communications). Youth + Labor dropped. Branding + Endorsement Process + Platform added as ad hoc. **§26 needs amendment.**

Plus minor:
- §3.4: 6-month wait period for Women's/Young Dems Club sanctioning — operational detail.
- §13.4: filing minutes with SCEC Chair only; KDP requires SCEC Chair + KDP Exec Director. Probably honored in practice; could update text.

These don't get silently fixed in any portal or operational copy. They get cited as the rule on the books, with the KDP/practice mismatch flagged.
